OPPO A37F


The OPPO A37F is an entry-level smartphone with a metal back and a 5-inch IPS LCD screen with HD resolution. The phone has an 8MP primary camera with autofocus and a 5MP selfie camera with a fixed focus lens. The OPPO A37F runs Android 5.1 Lollipop, and uses the Qualcomm Snapdragon 410 processor, 2GB RAM and 16GB storage. There’s a microSD card slot for storage expansion. It’s a dual-SIM phone with GPS, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, microUSB port and 3.5mm headphone port. The OPPO A37F has a 2630mAh battery. The other popular Oppo mobiles you can look for are the Oppo F7, Oppo A7 and the Oppo FInd X.
